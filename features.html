AppEx is a web-based planning and reporting tool for anyone with coordinative responsibilities in a project. AppEx can be used by individuals or companies.

Features

• AppEx is a web-based planning and reporting tool for anyone with coordinative responsibilities in a project. AppEx can be used by individuals or companies.

• AppEx is experience-based. It allows you plan activities based on the knowledge and experiences gained by you or people in your organization.

• AppEx allows you to record solutions to problems and document the quality of work products upon completion of project phases.

• AppEx shows status maps of projects, making the planning and QC status of projects very visible.

Experiences are displayed by phase and trade.

AppEx lets the user report on new experiences.

Benefits

• Very easy to use. Saves time in the preparation of estimates, submittals, and field QC documents

• Higher planning quality in early project phases results in

- More accurate submittals, cost estimates
- Fewer change orders, rework and claims

• Fosters collaboration, prevents repeat mistakes

• Helps you ask clients the important design questions early

• Document solutions and quality control of work products

• See project status at a glance, including potential risks and open issues

• Make experience actionable for future projects

• Because of its ease of use and relevance AppEx has daily uses and is front and center in a project.

• Always available - works on all mobile devices
