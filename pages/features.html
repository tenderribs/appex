About us

Applied Experience started in 2005 in Silicon Valley, CA and provided intranet design and the web-based knowledge management tool Connected Projects to general contractors and owners, several of them large, national firms.


The current iteration of Applied Experience is based in Switzerland and internationally active with its new and vastly improved tool AppEx.

How does it work?

• AppEx is a web-based planning and reporting tool for anyone with coordinative responsibilities in a project. AppEx can be used by individuals or companies.

• AppEx is experience-based. It allows you plan activities based on the knowledge and experiences gained by you or people in your organization.

• AppEx allows you to record solutions to problems and document the quality of work products upon completion of project phases.

• AppEx shows status maps of projects, making the planning and QC status of projects very visible.
